Kulasai Dussehra: Where Devotion Meets Spectacle

Journey to Kulasekarapattinam, a coastal town in Tamil Nadu, where Dussehra transcends festivity to become a mesmerizing spectacle of faith, mythology, and devotion. Unlike anywhere else, thousands of devotees transform into gods, demons, and celestial beings, honoring Goddess Mutharamman in a tradition that has endured for centuries.
